Owasso public schools hold almost 10,00 students with 15 campuses.

Recent construction has revitalized the downtown area making it a hub for thriving buisnesses.

The Owasso public library is a branch of the Tulsa Library system and houses over 45,000 books.

Neighborhoods here are clean and have many ameneties such as pools and playgrounds along with naturally beautiful ponds.

Tulsa Tech has created a college campus with a wide variety of classes in Owasso for those seeking a higher education.
